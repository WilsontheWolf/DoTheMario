Trouble Shooting:

Playing Audio/Joining Voice Channels

  • Make sure you are in a voice channel
  • Make sure the bot can join your voice channel.
  • Make sure that the bot can speak in this voice channel.
  • Make sure the bot is not in another voice channel.

Getting Stuck In A Voice Channel

  • Wait a bit. If it crashed it might take 40 seconds to disconnect from the channel.
  • Get a moderator to disconnect the bot manually.
